Methodology Notes

Trust Score

Weighted average of verification completeness, audit findings, and compliance coverage.

Community Wealth

Economic Value of Volunteerism + Circular Resource Value + Social Impact Monetization.

ZWTL (Zero Waste to Landfill)

Percentage based on verified waste diversion records submitted to authorized processors.

Assurance Levels

  • Reasonable: Independent third-party verification with comprehensive testing
  • Limited: Internal controls with external sample-based verification
  • Developing: Self-reported with verification infrastructure under development
